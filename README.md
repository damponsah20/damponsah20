## 🌍 About Me

I'm a **Teaching & Research Assistant** in the Department of Meteorology and Climate Science at **Kwame Nkrumah University of Science and Technology (KNUST)**, Ghana — working at the intersection of climate dynamics, public health, and applied data science.

- 🔭 **Currently researching:** heatwave prevalence, frequency, duration, and intensity across Ghana's climatic zones, using the **Excess Heat Factor (EHF)** framework and **GEV distribution**
- 🧪 **Also building:** a climate-health early warning dashboard forecasting malaria risk (seasonal cases, EIR, vector density) for CHRL Ghana
- 🌱 **Community work:** co-leading **Climate Cardinals KNUST Chapter**, translating climate resources into local Ghanaian languages
- 🤝 **Open to:** collaborations in climate science, geospatial analysis, ML for climate-health applications, and research writing
- 📫 **Reach me:** amponsahdaniel2020@gmail.com · damponsah11@st.knust.edu.gh
- ⚡ **Fun fact:** I never call a task done until there's a better version of it
