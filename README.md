## 🌍 &nbsp;About Me

> I am a **Teaching & Research Assistant** in the **Department of Meteorology and Climate Science** at **Kwame Nkrumah University of Science and Technology (KNUST)**, Ghana. My work sits at the intersection of **atmospheric science, environmental data science, and machine learning** — using observations, reanalysis, satellites and models to understand a changing climate and to translate that understanding into decisions that protect people, water and ecosystems.
